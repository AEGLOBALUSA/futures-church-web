# 3-minute walkthrough script — for Josh (or his delegated writer) to record

**Format:** Loom or QuickTime, screen-share. ~3 minutes. Send the link to all 21 pastors with the email above.

---

## Script

**[0:00 — title card / Josh's face]**

Hey team. This is Josh. I'm going to walk you through the 30-second version of how to fill in your campus on the new Futures website. Then you'll get an email from me with your unique link.

**[0:15 — share screen, open the unique link]**

When you click your link, you land here. This is your campus's intake form. Thirteen sections down the left side. Click the first one.

**[0:30 — click the first section]**

You'll see a form. Type into the boxes. **Everything autosaves as you type.** No save button. You can close the tab and come back tomorrow — your work is there.

**[0:50 — show the photo upload section]**

When you get to a photo section, drag a photo in or click to browse. **Photos go into a campus pool** — the comms team picks which ones go where. Don't worry about cropping or sizing.

**[1:10 — flip back to the table of contents]**

The progress bar at the top shows how complete your campus is. **Aim for 100% in 14 days.** Easier to do 2 sections a day than try to power through.

**[1:30 — show the most important section]**

The "**Distinctive**" section is the most important. One paragraph on what makes your campus your campus — written in YOUR voice, not church copy. That's what people will read, and what Milo (our website guide) will quote when someone asks about your campus.

**[1:50 — close on a calm shot]**

If you're stuck or unsure, text me directly. Don't guess.

**[2:10 — end card]**

If you can't do this yourself this week, **forward your link to anyone you trust** — campus admin, your spouse, your media person, anyone. They can finish it for you and the link still works for 30 days from first click.

**[2:30 — sign off]**

Two weeks. Let's get the site to launch with every campus represented properly.

Thanks for what you do.

[End — 2:45ish]

---

## Recording tips

- **Don't over-rehearse.** A slightly-stumbling Josh is more trusted than a polished read.
- **Show YOUR cursor on the screen as you click.** People follow movement.
- **Filename the recording:** `futures-intake-josh-walkthrough-2026-05.mp4`
- **Loom is fastest** — share the auto-generated URL in the email.
