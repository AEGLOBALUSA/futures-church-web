# Futures Staff Questionnaire — Campus Voice

**Who this is for:** every Lead Pastor couple (or campus coordinator) at every Futures and Futuros campus.

**Why it matters:** right now every campus page on the website uses the same copy — same welcome, same three "what to expect" bullet points, same everything. A visitor can't tell Paradise from Bali from Gwinnett. That's not who we are. The answers you give below land directly on your campus page so it reads like *your room*, not like a headquarters press release.

**How long:** ~20 minutes. Do it once, voice-note style. Don't overthink it.

---

## How to answer

For each of the five sections below, write in your own voice. Don't write "church copy." Write like you're texting a friend who just asked "what's your church like?"

- **Short sentences.** Nothing purple.
- **Specifics over generalities.** "The coffee van shows up at 9:15" beats "we love community."
- **Funny is fine.** Don't strain for it, but don't edit yourself dry.
- **Skip the Bible verses.** Not here. This is the doorway, not the sermon.

When you're done, email the completed doc to the Futures Global content team — or drop it in the shared drive folder `Campus Voice · 2026 Refresh/`.

## Section 2 — *Only at us* (2–3 bullets)

> These render as a short list on your page under the heading *"Only at [Your Campus]."*

Give us **two or three specifics** that no other Futures campus could honestly claim. Details. Names of things. Times of things. Street names. A tradition. A smell. A regular.

**Examples from other campuses (don't copy — write your own):**
- "The Paradise Roasters van shows up at 9:15 and we don't start without it."
- "Our worship team leads in Indonesian on Sunday nights — English only in the morning."
- "The back row is full of tradies who came straight from Saturday night shift."

**Your two or three bullets:**

```
- [insert specific 1]
- [insert specific 2]
- [insert specific 3 — optional]
```
