## Q4 · From Nick & Danielle (~80 words, first-person) · `pastorBio`

> Status: **EDITOR DRAFT** — awaiting Nick & Danielle's words

```
Danielle and I came to Gwinnett because we believed suburban Atlanta needed a
room where genuinely different kinds of people could sit next to each other.
That bet's paid off in ways we never expected. This campus runs people from
forty-plus countries, every age, every decade of faith — and it still feels
like a neighbourhood church. Come on a Sunday. If you leave wondering what just
happened, that's the right response.
```
